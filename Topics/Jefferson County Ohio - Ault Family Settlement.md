# Jefferson County, Ohio — Ault Family Settlement

## Overview

Jefferson County, Ohio (eastern Ohio, Ohio River valley region) served as a primary settlement location for the Ault family during the 1800s. The [[People/Andrew Ault|Andrew Ault]] patriarch established farming operations here in advanced age, documented in the 1850 US census. Jefferson County represents a secondary Ohio settlement zone compared to Sandusky County, with documented Ault family presence spanning the 1800s.
