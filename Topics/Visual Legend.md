# Visual Legend for Pedigree Timelines

This project utilizes a sophisticated notation system developed by Robert "Butch" Thorpe in his original CorelDRAW pedigree diagrams. These symbols provide high-signal indicators of available records and research status.

## Where to Find These Visuals
Every major ancestor in the vault now features a **Lifespan Snippet** at the very top of their page. This visual bar is extracted directly from the high-resolution pedigree charts and provides an at-a-glance view of their records.

- **[[People/Ralph Gerald Thorpe]]**: A complete record set including census hits, multiple primary records (red dots), and burial/obituary markers.

## Symbols and Indicators

### Lifespan Bars
![[assets/snippets/Ralph Gerald Thorpe.svg]]
- **Horizontal Bar**: Represents the verified or estimated lifespan of an ancestor.
- **Background Grid**: Vertical lines represent 50-year increments (1700, 1750, 1800, etc.).

### Record Indicators (Dots)
- <span style="color: #e30016; font-size: 1.5em;">●</span> **Solid Red Circle**: Indicates a **Primary Record or Certificate** (Birth, Death, or Marriage) is held in the Thorpe family archive. 
- **Reference Numbers**: Solid red dots are often accompanied by 3-digit numbers (e.g., `#052`, `#250`). These are found in the **Source Indicators** section of the person page and correspond to Butch's physical filing system.
- <span style="color: #e30016; font-size: 1.5em;">○</span> **Hollow Red Circle**: Indicates a possible certificate or record that has been identified but not yet obtained.
- <span style="color: #009ee0; font-size: 1.5em;">●</span> **Solid Blue Circle**: Indicates the individual was **Enumerated in the Census** for that year. The year is calculated based on the dot's horizontal position.
- <span style="color: #009ee0; font-size: 1.5em;">○</span> **Hollow Blue Circle**: Indicates a possible census match requiring further verification.

### Status Icons
- **RIP Marker**: The "RIP" icon indicates the **Burial Site** has been determined, often through physical visitation or cemetery record verification.
- **Obit Marker**: The "Obit" icon indicates a copy of the individual's **Obituary** is available in the archive.
