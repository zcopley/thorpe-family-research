# Family Stories and Biographies

This hub collects the narrative-driven accounts of the Thorpe family and connected lines. While much of this vault is dedicated to primary source data (census, pedigree, and burial records), these stories provide a window into the lived experiences of our ancestors.

## Featured Narratives

### The Civil War Service of Watson Moses Risden
The profile of **[[People/Watson Moses Risden|Watson Moses Risden]]** includes a detailed biographical account of his service in the 128th Indiana Volunteer Infantry, his participation in major battles across Georgia and Tennessee, and his eventual rise to Department Commander of the Iowa Grand Army of the Republic.
