## Verified vs Uncertain

- **Verified:** Pages 11-15 give the compiled older chain from Samuel Miller to John Miller and summarize Samuel Miller's children.
- **Verified:** Page 240 identifies Mathias Miller as a child of John Miller and Anna Smootz.
- **Verified:** Page 250 says Elvin Lenora Tallman married U. B. Thorpe on 22 October 1903 and had Ralph Thorpe, born 27 October 1906.
- **Verified:** The U. B. Thorpe birth date on page 250 matches [[People/Uriah Blake Thorpe|Uriah Blake Thorpe]]'s existing birth date.
- **Uncertain:** The source gives Samuel Miller's birth and marriage as deductions, not confirmed record extracts.
- **Uncertain:** `Lizzie Wimmer` may refer to [[People/Elizabeth Plomey Ault|Elizabeth Plomey Ault]], but this remains unproven.
- **Uncertain:** `William Gilmore Tallman` may refer to Willis G. Tallman, and `Elvin Lenora Tallman` may refer to [[People/Lenore Hetty Tallman|Lenore Hetty Tallman]], but primary records are still needed.
