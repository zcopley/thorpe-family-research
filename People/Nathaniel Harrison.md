# Nathaniel Harrison

## Biographical Profile

- **Name:** Nathaniel Harrison
- **Role in this project:** Harrison-line father of [[People/Elizabeth Harrison|Elizabeth Harrison]], whose marriage to [[People/John Tallman|John Tallman]] connects the Harrison material to the Tallman branch.

## Source-Cited Facts

- *Settlers by the Long Grey Trail* page 312 lists Nathaniel Harrison as a child of Capt. Reuben Harrison and first wife Lydia Harrison.
- Page 312 says Nathaniel Harrison died in 1807 and married [[People/Mary Woodley|Mary Woodley]] in 1784.
- Page 375 says Nathaniel Harrison died testate in 1807 and left no widow, but left children named Lydia, Phebe, Gracy, Anna, [[People/Elizabeth Harrison|Elizabeth]], Mary, Jerusha, Edith, Lucretia, Samuel, and Nathaniel.
- Page 375 says litigation over Nathaniel Harrison's heirs identified Elizabeth as wife of [[People/John Tallman|John Tallman]].
