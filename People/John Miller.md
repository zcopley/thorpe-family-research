# John Miller

## Biographical Profile

- **Name:** John Miller
- **Role in this project:** Compiled Miller-line ancestor and father of [[People/Mathias Miller|Mathias Miller]] in the Samuel Miller book outprint.

## Source-Cited Facts

- The *Genealogy and History of Samuel Miller* outprint lists John Miller as a child of [[People/Samuel Miller|Samuel Miller]].
- Page 15 says John Miller was born in York County, Pennsylvania, and moved with his parents to Rockingham County, Virginia, about 1782.
- Page 15 says John Miller married Anna Smootz.
- Page 15 says John Miller moved to Fairfield County, Ohio, in 1805 and settled about two miles south of Millersport, Ohio, on the farm later owned by Lewis Hite.
- Page 240 says John Miller married Anna Smootz and had four known children: [[People/Mathias Miller|Mathias Miller]], Anna Miller, Jacob Miller, and Polly Miller.

## Family Connections

- **Father:** [[People/Samuel Miller|Samuel Miller]], based on the compiled Miller genealogy.
- **Spouse:** Anna Smootz.
- **Child:** [[People/Mathias Miller|Mathias Miller]].
