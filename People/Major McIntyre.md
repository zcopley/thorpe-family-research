# Major McIntyre

## Biographical Profile

- **Name:** Major McIntyre
- **Role in this project:** McIntyre-line burial-book entry associated with Sarah Shutts McIntyre.

## Source-Cited Facts

- The Burial Sites book places Major McIntyre at Tew Cemetery in Townsend Township, Sandusky County, Ohio (page 19), Section 25-17-5, Lat. 412203N Long. 0825125W.
- The page gives a date of death of 28 July 1842 and inscription `Major Mcintyre / Died July 28 1842 / Æ 53 ys 2 m & 26d`.
- The same book comments that Sarah Shutts McIntyre’s stone, on the next page, is right next to Major McIntyre’s stone and may have been carved by the same stone carver.
- Map: [Google Maps](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Tew+Cemetery+Townsend+Township+Sandusky+County+Ohio).
- The MacIntyre book outprint lists Major as a child of [[People/William McIntyre|William McIntyre]] and [[People/Rosanna Campbell|Rosanna Campbell]] McIntyre, with birth date 3 May 1789.
- The 3 May 1789 birth date is consistent with the Burial Sites inscription age of 53 years, 2 months, and 26 days at death on 28 July 1842.
