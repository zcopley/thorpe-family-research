# Emilie J. Thorp

## Biographical Profile

- **Name:** Emilie J. Thorp
- **Role in this project:** Side-branch Thorp individual represented in the Burial Sites book.

## Source-Cited Facts

- The Burial Sites book lists Emilie J. Thorp on page 46 and places her at DeYo-Bolls Cemetery near I-80 and Ohio 269 in Erie County, Ohio, with Lat. 412034N Long. 0825000W.
- The same page identifies Emilie J. Thorp as the daughter of Monroe and Sarah Thorp.
- The inscription records `EMILIE J. / Dau. of / M. & S. A. THORP / DIED / Nov. 18, 1860 / AGED / 6m. 2w & 6d.`
- Map: [Google Maps](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=DeYo-Bolls+Cemetery+Erie+County+Ohio).
