# Elizabeth A Quackenbush

## Biographical Profile

- **Name:** Elizabeth A Quackenbush
- **Role in this project:** Indexed profile linked to Lewis-household census-summary extracts.

## Source-Cited Facts

- A census-summary entry gives Elizabeth A Quackenbush as born about 1836 and died 15 Dec 1909.
- The section includes 1860 Ripon, Fond du Lac County, Wisconsin Lewis-household entries and 1880 Grand Meadow Township, Mower County, Minnesota household entries where Elizabeth Lewis appears as spouse.
- The extracted rows cite M653 Roll 1408 (1860) and T9-0626 page 492D (1880).
- The Burial Sites book index lists Elizabeth Quackenbush as `1836-1909`, but no separate cemetery page was identified in the extracted text.
- The processed Prior timeline review keeps Elizabeth in the Lewis/Prior collateral zone but reads her date as `c1841-1909`, which should remain an open discrepancy.
