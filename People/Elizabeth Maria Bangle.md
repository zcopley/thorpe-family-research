# Elizabeth Maria Bangle

## Biographical Profile

- **Name:** Elizabeth Maria Bangle
- **Role in this project:** Direct ancestor indexed in census-summary pages with Bangle and Palmer-linked household entries.

## Source-Cited Facts

- A census-summary entry gives Elizabeth Maria Bangle as born 14 Sep 1859 and died 22 Nov 1945.
- The 1860 Bridge Creek Township, Eau Claire County, Michigan household includes an infant `Elisabeth BANGLE` in the Lewis and Angeline Bangle family.
- The 1870 Otter Creek Township, Eau Claire County, Wisconsin household includes `Elizabeth BANGLE`, age 11.
- The section also includes a 1900 Frankford Township, Mower County, Minnesota Palmer household table that includes a daughter `May PALMER`.
- The Burial Sites book places Elizabeth Maria Bangle at Fairview Cemetery in Waterloo, Iowa (page 4), Section M, Row 3, Grave 66, with date of death 22 November 1945 and inscription `PALMER / ELIZABETH M. / SEPT. 14, 1859 / NOV. 22, 1945 / MOTHER`. Map: [Google Maps](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Fairview+Cemetery+Waterloo+IA).
