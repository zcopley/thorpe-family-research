# Samuel Tallman

## Biographical Profile

- **Name:** Samuel Tallman
- **Role in this project:** Fairfield County, Ohio Tallman pioneer described in a local-history sketch.

## Source-Cited Facts

- *Pioneer People of Fairfield County, Ohio* says Samuel Tallman married [[People/Sarah Wells Tallman|Sarah Wells]] at Wellsburg, Virginia, on 28 March 1801.
- The same sketch says they moved from Wellsburg to Fairfield County, Ohio, in 1804.
- It describes Samuel as a carpenter and cabinetmaker who built many log houses in Greenfield Township.
- The sketch says Samuel died at the early age of 50 and that he and Sarah were buried beside her parents in the Wells family burying ground, later known as Hooker's.
