# Sarah Shutts

## Biographical Profile

- **Name:** Sarah Shutts
- **Role in this project:** McIntyre-line burial-book entry with an uncertain stone-reading.

## Source-Cited Facts

- The Burial Sites book places Sarah Shutts at Tew Cemetery in Townsend Township, Sandusky County, Ohio (page 31), Section 25-17-5, Lat. 412203N Long. 0825125W.
- The page gives a date-of-death field of 1 September 1854, but the inscription transcription appears to read `Sarah Mcintyre Died Sep 1, 1834 Æ 39 ys 3 mo & 16D`, and the author explicitly says the stone may not actually be Sarah Shutts McIntyre’s grave.
- Map: [Google Maps](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Tew+Cemetery+Townsend+Township+Sandusky+County+Ohio).
