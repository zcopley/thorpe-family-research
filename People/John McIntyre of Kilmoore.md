# John McIntyre of Kilmoore

## Biographical Profile

- **Name:** John McIntyre
- **Disambiguation:** Of Kilmoore, County Tyrone, Ireland.
- **Role in this project:** Older compiled McIntyre-line ancestor named in the MacIntyre book outprint.

## Source-Cited Facts

- The MacIntyre book outprint identifies John McIntyre as born in the parish of Kilmoore, County Tyrone, Ireland, about 1678.
- The same source says he came from Ireland before 1719.
- The source gives his death as 26 May 1759 at Rutland, Massachusetts.
- The source says he married Grace, who survived him.
- Pages 161-162 list children of John and Grace McIntyre, including Agnes, Janet, Elizabeth, Martha, Mary, Christian, John, James, and Thomas.
