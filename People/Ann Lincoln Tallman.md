# Ann Lincoln Tallman

## Biographical Profile

- **Name:** Ann Lincoln Tallman
- **Role in this project:** Older Tallman-line spouse identified in the Durfee genealogy as mother of [[People/Benjamin Tallman 1745-1820|Benjamin Tallman]].

## Source-Cited Facts

- *The Descendants of Thomas Durfee* volume II page 571 says Ann Lincoln married [[People/William Tallman 1720-1791|William Tallman]] in Berks County, Pennsylvania, on 20 October 1740 or 1742.
- Page 571 identifies Ann as a daughter of Mordecai Lincoln, son of Mordecai Lincoln and Sarah Jones Lincoln.
- Page 571 says Ann was born probably in Chester County, Pennsylvania, on 8 March 1725.
- Page 571 says Ann died on the farm near Harrisonburg, Virginia, on 22 December about 1812 and was buried there beside her husband.

## Family Connections

- **Spouse:** [[People/William Tallman 1720-1791|William Tallman]]
- **Child in this project:** [[People/Benjamin Tallman 1745-1820|Benjamin Tallman 1745-1820]]
