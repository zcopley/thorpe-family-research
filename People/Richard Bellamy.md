# Richard Bellamy

## Biographical Profile

- **Name:** Richard Bellamy
- **Role in this project:** Core Bellamy-line ancestor with repeated 1841-1881 Lincolnshire census-summary entries.

## Source-Cited Facts

- A census-summary entry gives Richard Bellamy as baptized 18 Mar 1816 and died 24 May 1902.
- The 1841 Bourn entry includes Richard Bellamy with James Bellamy at Castle Yard.
- The 1851, 1861, and 1871 Bourn entries list Richard Bellamy as household head with wife Mary and children including William, Thomas, George, Ann, Henry, and Alice across different years.
- The 1871 entry is explicitly cited in census-citation notes as RG10 Piece 3314, Folio 65, Page 5.
- The Bellamy pedigree timeline places Richard Bellamy in the Bellamy branch immediately after James Bellamy.
