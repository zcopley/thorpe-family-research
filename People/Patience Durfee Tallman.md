# Patience Durfee Tallman

## Biographical Profile

- **Name:** Patience Durfee Tallman
- **Role in this project:** Durfee-line spouse connecting the older Tallman line to *The Descendants of Thomas Durfee*.

## Source-Cited Facts

- *The Descendants of Thomas Durfee* volume I page 31 says Patience Durfee was a daughter of Thomas Durfee and his second wife, Deliverance Hall/Tripp Durfee.
- The same page says she was born in Portsmouth, Rhode Island, and died in Portsmouth in 1723.
- The same page says she married [[People/Benjamin Tallman 1684-1769|Benjamin Tallman]] at Portsmouth, Rhode Island, on 23 September 1708.
