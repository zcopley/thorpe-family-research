# Lenore Hetty Tallman

## Biographical Profile

- **Dates:** 1 Feb 1879 - 28 Jun 1953

- **Name:** Lenore Hetty Tallman
- **Role in this project:** Tallman-line descendant appearing as daughter in late-19th and early-20th century census-summary entries.

## Source-Cited Facts

- A census-summary entry gives Lenore Hetty Tallman as born 1 Feb 1879 and died 28 Jun 1953.
- The 1880 Afton Township, Cherokee County, Iowa extract lists Lena Tallman as daughter, age 1.
- The 1900 Sioux City, Woodbury County, Iowa extract lists Lena H. Tallman as daughter, born Feb 1879, age 21, single.
- The Burial Sites book places Lenore Hetty Tallman at Evergreen Cemetery in Grand Mound, Iowa (page 37), with date of death 28 June 1953. Map: [Google Maps](https://www.google.com/maps/search/?api=1&query=Evergreen+Cemetery+Grand+Mound+IA).
- The *Genealogy and History of Samuel Miller* page 242 lists Elvin Lenora Tallman, born 7 February 1879, as a child of [[People/Miller Mathias Tallman|Mathias M. Tallman]] and Lizzie Wimmer; the same page separately summarizes this child as Lenore Tallman.
- Page 250 of the same source says Elvin Lenora Tallman married [[People/Uriah Blake Thorpe|U. B. Thorpe]] on 22 October 1903 and had a child Ralph Thorpe born 27 October 1906.

## Research Gaps

1. Confirm whether Lena/Lena H./Lenore Hetty in compiled entries all refer to the same person.
2. Extend record coverage beyond 1900 and reconcile later-name variants.
3. Confirm parent-child links from primary census images.
4. Reconcile the exact birth date discrepancy: census/burial summaries give 1 February 1879, while the Miller genealogy gives 7 February 1879 for Elvin Lenora Tallman.
