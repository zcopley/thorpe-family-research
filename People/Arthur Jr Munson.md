# Arthur Jr Munson

## Biographical Profile

- **Name:** Arthur Jr Munson
- **Role in this project:** Essex Munson-line ancestor represented in 1841 and 1851 census-summary extracts.

## Source-Cited Facts

- A census-summary entry gives Arthur Jr Munson as baptized 24 Oct 1788 and died 6 Nov 1855.
- The 1841 Great Holland, Essex entry (`In the Green`) lists Arther Monson with household members including Matilda Monson.
- The 1851 Great Holland entry lists Arthur Munson (widower) with granddaughter Matilda Munson.
- The 1841 citation includes HO107 class/piece/book/folio/page/line metadata in the extract.

## Research Gaps
