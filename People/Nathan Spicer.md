# Nathan Spicer

## Biographical Profile

- **Name:** Nathan Spicer
- **Birth/Death:** Born 3 Apr 1796; died 16 May 1873 (age 77 years, 1 month, 13 days)
- **Role in this project:** Spicer line patriarch; grandfather of George B. Spicer; patriarch of Iowa Spicer agricultural line (1850s-1870s).
- **Burial:** Spicer/Spooner Cemetery, Homestead, Iowa; Grave 13 on Highway 6, 1.5 miles east; inscription `NATHAN SPICER / DIED / May 16, 1873 / AGED / 77yrs. 1mo. 13ds`; Burial Sites book, page 34.

## Census Records and Household Context

## Burial and Legacy

- **Burial:** Spicer/Spooner Cemetery, Homestead, Iowa (same cemetery where [[People/George B Spicer|George B. Spicer]]'s wife [[People/Hattie May Risden|Hattie May Risden]] was eventually buried)
- **Legacy:** Patriarch of Spicer line spanning 77 years (1796-1873); established in Iowa by 1850; sons continued agricultural prosperity (William with 1500-acre farm by 1860)

## Three-Generation Spicer Progression

1. **Nathan Spicer (1796-1873):** Patriarch, emigrated from New York/Ohio; established in Iowa by 1850
2. **Charles Russell Spicer (1822-1887):** Son; farmer, chair maker, bridge generation
3. **George B. Spicer (1864-1938):** Grandson; major patriarch with large household, 7+ children; prosperous farmer
