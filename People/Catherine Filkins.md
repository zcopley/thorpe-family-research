# Catherine Filkins

## Biographical Profile

- **Name:** Catherine Filkins
- **Role in this project:** Johnson/Spicer-linked ancestor represented in 1850 and 1860 New York census-summary entries.

## Source-Cited Facts

- A census-summary entry gives Catherine Filkins as born 5 Jan 1766 and died about 1868.
- The 1850 Nunda, Livingston County, New York table lists Catherine Johnson with Charles Johnson.
- The 1860 West Sparta, Livingston County, New York table includes Catherine Johnson in an Asa and Betsy Spicer household.
- The 1850 extract cites Series M432, Roll 524, Page 28, and the 1860 extract cites M653, Roll 779, Page 619 & 620.
