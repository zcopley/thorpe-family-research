# Samuel Miller

## Biographical Profile

- **Name:** Samuel Miller
- **Role in this project:** Older compiled Miller-line ancestor in the *Genealogy and History of Samuel Miller* outprint.

## Source-Cited Facts

- The *Genealogy and History of Samuel Miller* outprint says Samuel Miller was probably born in York County, Pennsylvania, about 1738, while noting that no definite birth record had been found.
- The same source says Samuel Miller married Magdalena Wiley about 1758 or 1759, but presents this as a deduction rather than a record-backed fact.
- The source places Samuel Miller in York County, Pennsylvania, tax records in 1777.
- The source says Samuel Miller purchased land in Rockingham County, Virginia, in 1782, about two miles west of Harrisonburg.
- The source says Samuel Miller probably died in 1789.
- The source lists known children as David, Elizabeth, Christly, Michael, Abraham, Barbara, [[People/John Miller|John]], Anna, Samuel, and Magdalena.

## Family Connections

- **Spouse:** Magdalena Wiley, based on the compiled Miller genealogy.
- **Child:** [[People/John Miller|John Miller]].
