# Hannah Beneworth

## Biographical Profile

- **Dates:** 9 Aug 1801 - 6 Apr 1866

- **Name:** Hannah Beneworth
- **Role in this project:** Thorogood-linked ancestor represented in 1841 and 1861 Felsted, Essex census-summary entries.

## Source-Cited Facts

- A census-summary entry gives Hannah Beneworth as born 9 Aug 1801 and died 6 Apr 1866.
- The 1841 Felsted entry includes Hannah Thorogood in a Joseph Thorogood household with children William, Mary Ann, Henry, and Frederick.
- The 1861 Felsted Village entry lists Hannah Thorogood as household head, occupation laundress.
