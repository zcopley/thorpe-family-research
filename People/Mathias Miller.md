# Mathias Miller

## Biographical Profile

- **Name:** Mathias Miller
- **Role in this project:** Miller-line household head represented in 1850-1870 Walnut Township, Ohio census-summary entries.

## Source-Cited Facts

- A census-summary heading records Mathias Miller with dates `7 Jan 1879 - 4 May 1871`, which appear to contain a birth-year transcription error.
- The profile section includes 1850, 1860, and 1870 Walnut Township, Fairfield County, Ohio household tables with Mathias Miller and family members.
- The extracts cite M432 Roll 677 Page 197 (1850), M653 Roll 961 Page 247 (1860), and M593 Roll 1198 Page 387B (1870).
- The *Genealogy and History of Samuel Miller* pages 11-15 present an older compiled Miller chain from [[People/Samuel Miller|Samuel Miller]] to [[People/John Miller|John Miller]].
- Page 240 identifies Mathias Miller as a child of [[People/John Miller|John Miller]] and Anna Smootz.
- The *Genealogy and History of Samuel Miller* page image for page 240 gives Mathias Miller as born 7 Jan 1789 and died 4 May 1871.
- The same page says Mathias Miller was born in Rockingham County, Virginia; moved with his parents to Fairfield County, Ohio, about 1805; married Amy E. Critton about 1818; owned land in and around Millersport; was a founder of the town; and was a contractor in digging the Ohio Canal.
