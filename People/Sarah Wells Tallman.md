# Sarah Wells Tallman

## Biographical Profile

- **Name:** Sarah Wells Tallman
- **Role in this project:** Fairfield County, Ohio pioneer and spouse of [[People/Samuel Tallman|Samuel Tallman]].

## Source-Cited Facts

- *Pioneer People of Fairfield County, Ohio* says Sarah Wells married [[People/Samuel Tallman|Samuel Tallman]] at Wellsburg, Virginia, on 28 March 1801.
- The sketch says she and Samuel moved to Fairfield County, Ohio, in 1804.
- It describes Sarah as extending hospitality and possessing notable energy and business foresight.
- The sketch says Sarah came to Ohio with her brother-in-law William Tallman on a difficult journey where each carried a child, and that the gray horse she rode had belonged to her companion.

## Family Connections

- **Spouse:** [[People/Samuel Tallman|Samuel Tallman]]
- **Children named in the sketch:** William, Susannah Harrison, Amrah Scothern, and Phebe Hooker
