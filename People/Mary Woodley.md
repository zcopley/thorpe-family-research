# Mary Woodley

## Biographical Profile

- **Name:** Mary Woodley
- **Role in this project:** Harrison-line mother of [[People/Elizabeth Harrison|Elizabeth Harrison]], connecting the Tallman branch to the Rockingham County Harrison/Woodley material in *Settlers by the Long Grey Trail*.

## Source-Cited Facts

- *Settlers by the Long Grey Trail* page 312 says [[People/Nathaniel Harrison|Nathaniel Harrison]] married Mary Woodley in 1784 and gives her birth date as 18 February 1764.
- Page 374 identifies Mary as a daughter of Jacob Woodley.
- Page 375 identifies Jacob Woodley's wife as Grace Looker and lists Mary, also called Polly, among their children.
- Page 375 says Mary married [[People/Nathaniel Harrison|Nathaniel Harrison]].
