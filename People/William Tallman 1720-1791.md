# William Tallman 1720-1791

## Biographical Profile

- **Name:** William Tallman
- **Role in this project:** Older Tallman-line ancestor identified in the Durfee genealogy as father of [[People/Benjamin Tallman 1745-1820|Benjamin Tallman]].

## Source-Cited Facts

- *The Descendants of Thomas Durfee* volume II page 571 says William Tallman was the fourth child of [[People/Benjamin Tallman 1684-1769|Benjamin Tallman]] and [[People/Patience Durfee Tallman|Patience Durfee Tallman]].
- Page 571 says William was born at Portsmouth, Rhode Island, on 25 March 1720.
- Page 571 says he died on his farm near Harrisonburg, Virginia, on 13 February 1791.
- Page 571 says he married [[People/Ann Lincoln Tallman|Ann Lincoln]] in Berks County, Pennsylvania, on 20 October 1740 or 1742.
- Page 572 says William's Bible record was copied into the Durfee genealogy but had damaged margins, so some dates were verified or supplemented from other sources.

## Family Connections

- **Parents:** [[People/Benjamin Tallman 1684-1769|Benjamin Tallman 1684-1769]] and [[People/Patience Durfee Tallman|Patience Durfee Tallman]]
- **Spouse:** [[People/Ann Lincoln Tallman|Ann Lincoln Tallman]]
- **Child in this project:** [[People/Benjamin Tallman 1745-1820|Benjamin Tallman 1745-1820]]
