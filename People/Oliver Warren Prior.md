# Oliver Warren Prior

## Biographical Profile

- **Name:** Oliver Warren Prior
- **Role in this project:** Prior branch household head represented in 1900-1930 census-summary entries.

## Source-Cited Facts

- A census-summary entry gives Oliver Warren Prior as born 15 Mar 1880 and died 29 May 1949.
- The 1900 Grand Meadows Township, Mower County, Minnesota entry lists Oliver W. Prior as son in Arthur and Martha Prior's household.
- The 1910 East Waterloo Township, 1920 Cedar Rapids, and 1930 Cedar Rapids entries track Oliver and May Prior households with multiple children.
