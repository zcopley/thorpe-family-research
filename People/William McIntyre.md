# William McIntyre

## Biographical Profile

- **Name:** William McIntyre
- **Role in this project:** Compiled McIntyre-line ancestor and documented father of [[People/Major McIntyre|Major McIntyre]] in the MacIntyre book outprint.

## Source-Cited Facts

- The MacIntyre book outprint identifies William McIntyre as probably a son of Thomas and Sarah McIntyre.
- The same source gives William McIntyre as born 1 March 1754.
- The MacIntyre outprint says William McIntyre married [[People/Rosanna Campbell|Rosanna Campbell]] at Chester, Massachusetts, on 25 July 1778, and states that Rosanna was born 14 January 1756.
- The source places William McIntyre in the 1790 census at Plantation 7, Hampshire County, Massachusetts.
- The source lists William and [[People/Rosanna Campbell|Rosanna Campbell]] McIntyre's children as Betsey, Thomas, William, Zimri, Anna, [[People/Major McIntyre|Major]], and Ananias.
- The same child list gives Major McIntyre as born 3 May 1789.
- The MacIntyre outprint says William McIntyre served in Captain Nathan Rowlee's company at Ticonderoga from 25 December 1776 to 3 April 1777; enlisted 9 July 1777; served 39 days on a march to reinforce the Northern Army; served seven days in August 1777 with Captain Enoch Shepard's company on a Bennington alarm; and served one month from 21 September 1777 on a Saratoga expedition.

## Family Connections

- **Possible parents:** Thomas McIntyre and Sarah McIntyre, based on the compiled MacIntyre source's "probably son" wording.
- **Spouse:** [[People/Rosanna Campbell|Rosanna Campbell]].
- **Child:** [[People/Major McIntyre|Major McIntyre]].
