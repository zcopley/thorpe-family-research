# James Munson

## Biographical Profile

- **Name:** James Munson
- **Role in this project:** Essex Munson-line ancestor represented across 1841-1901 census-summary extracts.

## Source-Cited Facts

- A census-summary entry gives James Munson as born 21 Oct 1814 and died 3 Apr 1904.
- The 1841 Great Holland rectory entry lists James Monson as male servant.
- The 1861-1901 Felsted/Felstead entries track James Munson in police constable, school attendance officer, and pensioner roles with wife Mary A. and children.
- The section includes cited references for HO107, RG9, RG10, RG11, RG12, and RG13 entries.
