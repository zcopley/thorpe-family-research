# Home

This page is the secondary orientation page for the Thorpe family research vault. For the public starting point, begin at [[index|Thorpe Family History]].

## Project Scope

| Metric | Coverage |
|--------|----------|
| **Individuals documented** | 111 people across primary family lines |
| **Primary branches** | Lemmon-Blake-Thorpe, Spicer-Risden, Palmer-Prior-Lewis, Ault-Tallman |
| **Time span** | 1760s-present, with strongest sourced coverage from 1841-1930 |

## Current Work

The first major identity reconciliation cycle is complete: the vault resolved or separated the initial seven uncertain identities tracked in [[Topics/Identity Reconciliation Matrix|Identity Reconciliation Matrix]]. Recent work has shifted toward enriching the strongest family clusters with detailed census household context and visitor-friendly branch navigation.

Every substantive claim should remain tied to a visible source. Pages that rely on compiled timelines or OCR-derived extracts should keep research gaps explicit until image-level verification is complete.
