# Thorpe Family Research Vault

Welcome to the Thorpe family research vault. This project is separate from the Copley family vault and is focused on building a source-grounded Thorpe genealogy.

## Scope

- Build Thorpe family person profiles from primary or near-primary records
- Track source provenance and confidence levels
- Preserve direct source files and extraction notes
- Cross-link to Copley research only where family lines intersect
