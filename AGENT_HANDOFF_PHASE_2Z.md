## Verified vs Uncertain

- **Verified:** Volume II pages 571-572 identify William Tallman and Ann Lincoln as parents of Benjamin Tallman 1745-1820.
- **Verified:** Volume II page 572 gives Benjamin Tallman 1745-1820 migration and military-service context and names Dinah Boone as his wife.
- **Verified:** Volume II page 572 gives Dinah Boone's baptism and death context and calls her a first cousin of Daniel Boone.
- **Uncertain:** The Durfee source is compiled genealogy; cited Bible, military, church, and Boone manuscript records still need direct review.
- **Uncertain:** William Tallman and Ann Lincoln's marriage year is printed as `1740-2`.
- **Uncertain:** Dinah Boone's birth day is ambiguous in the source text as `3-10 May 1749`.
